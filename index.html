About Us

Kisura Pepea is a groundbreaking initiative dedicated to empowering women in rural East Africa by addressing critical issues such as Gender-Based Violence (GBV) and the impacts of climate change. Through creative storytelling, education, and advocacy, we aim to build resilient communities where women are empowered, informed, and actively contributing to sustainable development.

Mission

To empower women in rural East Africa by addressing Gender-Based Violence (GBV) and climate change through creative storytelling, community engagement, and sustainable solutions that amplify voices, promote equality, and inspire action.

Vision

A future where women in rural Africa thrive in safe, equitable, and sustainable communities, driving transformative change for themselves and their environment.

Quick topics:

Community-led resilience hubs, ecosystem restoration, and climate-smart livelihoods that reduce risk and improve food security.
Confidential counseling, legal referrals, safety planning and targeted outreach to survivors during climate emergencies.
Policy campaigns and research connecting climate justice and gender equity; we build evidence led proposals for funders.

🌱 Climate Action

Mobile training, reforestation, and resilience planning in vulnerable regions.

🛡️ Survivor Support

Counseling, safety planning, legal referrals, and community awareness programs.

🗣️ Community Voice

Amplifying local leadership and building inclusive solutions that last.

Our mission

We empower women, girls, and marginalized communities impacted by climate emergencies, while protecting ecosystems and promoting gender-sensitive climate resilience.

  • ✦ Build safe, resilient habitats
  • ✦ Provide trauma-informed care
  • ✦ Train local changemakers
  • ✦ Advocate for policy that protects people and nature

Programs

🏘️

Community resilience hubs with solar, water and safe spaces.

Local centers that provide emergency shelter, green jobs training, and safety planning for vulnerable groups.

💚

Trauma-informed services and referral networks.

Confidential counseling, case management, and referral pathways to legal aid.

📢

Data-driven advocacy that links climate and gender policy.

We produce evidence briefs and run policy dialogues with local leaders.
